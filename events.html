Annual MNC MEeting
Each spring the MNC has an annual membership meeting and executive board elections annually. This meeting takes place at the Manette Community Church. This is a great time to come meet your neighbors and get active in your neighborhood. Often these meetings have a guest speaker or presentation. Do you have a question? Want to join our mailing list? 
Check our MNC FB page for date and time of all our board meetings.

Annual Garage Sale
Come on over to Manette for the annual community garage sale that takes place the 3rd Saturday in July!  More than 30 households and businesses participated this year in a veritable shopping extravaganza in the friendly and picturesque community of Manette. Want to participate next year? There will be a call for participants on the Manette Neighborhood Coalition facebook page. Or, contact us here. 

Edible Garden Tour & Tour De Coop
Check out the website:
 www.manetteediblegardens.com
The simi-annual Manette Edible Garden Tour de Coop is sponsored by the Manette Neighborhood Coalition. This event takes place the first Saturday in August in even-numbered years. 

Come visit local gardens and see how we grow our own organic food in an urban environment. You will see many approaches to growing fresh food year round and to restoring the health of your family, your land and the Puget Sound.
Demonstrations include winter vegetable gardening, fruit-bearing trees and plants
in small spaces, extending the growing season, container gardening, hens, coops and chicken tractors, and greenhouses and hoophouses.

The MNC Garden Tour Coordinators will be recruiting gardeners and chickenistas to participate in the tour. If you would like to have your garden considered for the tour send and email to Manetteediblegardens@gmail.com.

Winter Fest
Winter Fest happens each December in Bremerton. It consists of three days of holiday cheer!

Downtown Bremerton celebrates on Friday,
There is Magic in Manette on Saturday, and Charleston has more festivities on Sunday!

The Manette business district will host Santa, kids activities, kettle corn, music, dancing, and the big tree lighting! Shops and restaurants are open late so you can pick up holiday gifts and treats!
