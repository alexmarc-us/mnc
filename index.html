Welcome!

The Manette Neighborhood Coalition was originally formed in response to the sale of the old Manette grade school on 13th and Hayward. Neighbors joined together and presented their concerns to the Bremerton School Board.

Grassroots efforts are very effective when organized and presented within the development process. Future projects of the Manette Neighborhood Coalition will involve the new City of Bremerton re-zoning sub area plans and creating/retaining park and open air spaces. The purpose of the Coalition is to gather input, share information, and voice our concerns for the future of our neighborhood.

Manette is OUR neighborhood. We need to be involved in City development decisions. Collectively we can see that Manette is developed in a way that is conducive to the existing neighborhood. The manette Neighborhood Coalition is an all volunteer, non-profit organization.

Are you interested in being part of a neighborhood coalition and having a voice in what happens in Manette? Get involved today! 

Coalition Mission Statement:

The Manette Neighborhood Coaltion is an autonomous organization that actively provides an effective, focused voice for the residential neighborhood of Manette. Our purpose is to gather and disseminate information and, when deemed necessary, act on matters that affect the neighborhood in which we live.
